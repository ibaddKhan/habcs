About Us

HABC was established in 2015 with a mission to demonstrate highly professional skilled manpower in the field of internal and external cleaning projects. We provide highly skilled professionals with extensive experience in cleaning and civil engineering. We've been in the business of building long enough to know building cleaning inside and outside, from top to bottom.

Our team has access to the latest in cleaning equipment and qualified staff for housekeeping, horticulture, and pest control, enabling us to offer unparalleled international quality standards. We can handle services for internal & external areas with full backup support from our international equipment & chemicals.
